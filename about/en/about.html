About us

Welcome to Smartguide Holzkirchen – a bilingual website (English & German) that introduces you to the town of Holzkirchen in a modern, creative, and personal way!

We are students from the Staatliches Gymnasium Holzkirchen and have developed this website from scratch as part of our P-Seminar project. Our goal was to present Holzkirchen from a fresh perspective – informative, digital, user-friendly, and visually appealing!



🖥️ Our project:

We are already the second group of students to take on this project as part of the P-Seminar. Building on the impressive first version created a few years ago, we carefully analyzed its structure and content. Our aim was to take the website to the next level – improving it in terms of content, design, and of course, the technical aspects. We wanted to bring in fresh ideas and a modern look.

The website is bilingual (English & German) to offer both locals and international visitors easy and enjoyable access to useful information about Holzkirchen.

📈Our process:

Over several months, we worked intensively as a team – from initial planning to final implementation. To manage the tasks efficiently, we organized ourselves into three specialized groups:

Coding-Team
Responsible for the technical implementation using GitHub Pages, HTML, CSS, and other tools.

Research-Team
In charge of gathering relevant information from Holzkirchen – such as restaurants, shops, and cultural activities – and writing all content in both English and German.

Design-Team
Responsible for the layout, the visual design, icons, and the overall structure of the website.

🛍️ Our content:

The website is divided into four main categories:
  • Food & Drinks – Local restaurants, cafés, and culinary recommendations
  • Shopping – Stores, markets, and shopping opportunities
  • Culture – Cultural places, events, and leisure activities
  • Surprise Me! – A randomizer button that shows you something new each time you click

🔧 Technical Background:

The entire website was developed using GitHub Pages – a setup that allowed us to gain real experience in web development. From the first commit to the final launch, we planned, designed, and implemented everything ourselves.
