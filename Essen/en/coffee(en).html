Cafés und Bistros

There are some Holzkirchner Cafés and Eisdielen for an unforgettable time with friends.

Eiscafé Crema Gelato ▼

Adress:Münchnerstraße 38, 83607 Markt Holzkirchen

Phone:08024 91218

"Crema Gelato offers you authentic, handmade ice cream right in Holzkirchen. At the ice cream parlor on the main street, you can enjoy a wide selection of delicious, fresh gelato flavors – from classic varieties to creative innovations. Perfect for a sweet break or a refreshing treat during a walk through Holzkirchen. Every scoop is made with love and highquality ingredients."

Eiscafé Franzetti ▼

Adress:Münchner Str. 7, 83607 Markt Holzkirchen

Phone:0172 9039384

"At Franzetti Ice Cream Parlor, you can enjoy freshly made ice cream crafted from the finest ingredients – without preservatives. In addition to ice cream, the shop offers specialty coffee in collaboration with Dinzler Coffee Roastery, as well as delicious cakes and tarts. In winter, Franzetti transforms into a cozy meeting place with homemade punch and hearty snacks."

Abfüllerei & Café Holzkirchen-Unverpackt ▼

Adress:Münchner Straße 27, 83607 Markt Holzkirchen

Phone:0176 80535021

"The Abfüllerei offers plastic-free, regional, and fair-trade organic food as well as sustainable household goods. The selection is complemented by a daytime café serving small dishes and high-quality coffee. As a meeting place for exchange and projects, the Abfüllerei focuses on community and sustainability"

Café Bistro Weinbar Papst ▼

Adress:Münchner Str. 56a, 83607 Markt Holzkirchen

Phone:08024 6086386

"Café Bistro Papst in the Atrium Holzkirchen is a popular place to relax. With its modern design and elegant atmosphere, it provides the perfect setting for enjoying breakfast, a delicious lunch, or a pleasant evening with friends. The café also offers a catering service to enhance your events with delicious food. A place to simply feel good."
