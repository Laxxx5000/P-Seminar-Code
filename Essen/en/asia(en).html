Asian

Here are some asian restaurants in Holzkirchen, that might be worth your time.

Asiaküche ▼

Address: Münchner Straße 5, 83607 Markt Holzkirchen

Phone: 08024 4674690

Asiaküche brings together specialties from Japan, China, Thailand, and Vietnam. From fresh sushi to spicy curries and Vietnamese noodle soups, the restaurant offers authentic dishes made with high-quality ingredients. A perfect place for gourmets and lovers of Asian flavors.

Masala ▼

Address: Müchnerstraße 38, 83607 Markt Holzkirchen

Phone: 08024 4745257

For many years, MASALA Restaurant has been delighting guests in Holzkirchen with authentic Indian cuisine. Visitors can immerse themselves in the exotic world of Indian flavors and enjoy a selection of fine, aromatic specialties. The best delicacies from India are served in a cozy atmosphere, all prepared with love and dedication.

Tam Nhu Vietnamesische Küche und Sushi Bar Café ▼

Address: Münchner Straße 5, 83607 Markt Holzkirchen

Phone: 08024 4752475

Tam Nhu invites guests to immerse themselves in the flavors of traditional Vietnamese cuisine. The restaurant offers a variety of dishes prepared with fresh ingredients and rice, inspired by Vietnam’s tropical monsoon climate. Guests can enjoy intense, diverse flavors and an authentic culinary experience. The cozy atmosphere, along with a sushi bar and café, completes the offering.
