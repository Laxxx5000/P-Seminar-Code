Bavarian Restaurants

Here you can find Bavarian restaurants for a nice dinner in Holzkirchen.

Zum Oberbräu Holzkirchen ▼

Adress: Marktplatz 18, 83607 Markt Holzkirchen

Phone: 08024 9009242

Enjoy German specialties at our charming restaurant, set in a romantic location perfect for cozy moments. In our outdoor seating area, you can fully embrace the summer atmosphere. We offer a wide selection of delicious dishes, from hearty meals to fresh salads, as well as delightful desserts like ice cream and pancakes. Vegetarian options and healthy meals are also part of our menu. Visit us for lunch or dinner and let yourself be indulged!

Hotel und Gasthof "Alte Post" ▼

Adress: Marktplatz 10a, 83607 Markt Holzkirchen

Phone: 08024 30050

The Alte Post in Holzkirchen has been run by the Falke & Huber family since 1997 and is now in its fourth generation. With warm Bavarian hospitality and 43 comfortable rooms, the inn provides a welcoming home for both hotel and day guests. Here, tradition meets modern service, giving every stay a special touch.

Gasthof Oberland ▼

Adress: Münchner Straße 18, 83607 Markt Holzkirchen

Phone: 08024 4602350

Gasthof Oberland offers a wide selection of freshly prepared dishes – from pizza and pasta to schnitzel and Balkan grill specialties. Quality meets warm service here to ensure every guest has a pleasant experience. Whether dining in a cozy setting or ordering delivery to your home – enjoy delicious food just the way you like it.

machtSinn ▼

Adress: Raiffeisenstraße 8, 83607 Markt Holzkirchen

Phone: 08024 6088924

machtSINN offers a wide selection of regional products, fresh dishes, and delicacies, all prepared with great care and 100% regional ingredients. Guests can not only browse for their daily shopping but also enjoy delicious meals freshly prepared before their eyes. All dishes are also available to go, allowing regional flavors to be enjoyed anywhere.
