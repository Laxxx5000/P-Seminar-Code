Döner Kebab

Fancy a kebab? Luckily there are 4 stores in Holzkirchen. One better than the other!

Irems Kebap ▼

Adress: Münchner Str. 49, 83607 Markt Holzkirchen

Phone:08024 6081150

"At Irem’s Kebap, guests can enjoy homemade Turkish specialties, prepared fresh, fast, and delicious – perfect for a quick meal or a relaxed gathering. In addition to tasty wraps, kebabs, and fast-food classics, the menu also includes vegan, vegetarian, and halal options. The selection is completed with desserts and refreshing beverages."

King Kebab ▼

Adress: Industriestr. 9, 83607 Markt Holzkirchen

Phone:0163 4895296

"King Kebap offers delicious döner, dürüm, burgers, and refreshing drinks – perfect for a quick bite. All dishes are freshly prepared and made with high-quality ingredients. Ideal for a lunch break or an evening snack, available for takeout or a short stop on-site."

YARAR Kebaphaus Holzkirchen ▼

Adress: Münchner Straße 63, 83607 Markt Holzkirchen

Phone: 08024 4709954

"At Yarar Kebap Haus, guests can enjoy freshly prepared döner, dürüm, and kebabs, perfect for a quick meal. All dishes are made with high-quality ingredients and lots of care. Juicy specialties can be enjoyed in a cozy setting or easily taken to go."

Kebap und Grillhähnchen Imbiss ▼

Adress:Bahnhofplatz 9, 83607 Markt Holzkirchen

Phone:0176 58873776

"At the Kebap and Grilled Chicken Snack Bar, guests can enjoy delicious kebabs, dürüm, grilled chicken, and more – all freshly prepared and perfect for a quick bite on the go. Ideal for those with little time who still want quality and great taste."
