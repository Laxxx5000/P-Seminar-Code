Bakeries

Craving for a healthy breakfast or a snack? The bakeries in Holzkirchen are the perfect place to fulfill your desires.

Bäckerei Hofpfisterei Holzkirchen ▼

Address: Münchner Str. 9-11, 83607 Markt Holzkirchen

Phone:08024 478412

With nearly 700 years of history, Hofpfisterei combines Bavarian baking tradition with ecological commitment. Its farmer’s bread is made exclusively from organic grains without artificial additives. As a pioneer in environmental protection, the bakery has been publishing annual environmental reports since 1991 and actively supports sustainable agriculture. As a Naturland partner, Hofpfisterei adheres to the strictest organic standards – for a healthy and eco-friendly future.

Bäckerei Hohenadl ▼

Address: Münchner Str. 36, 83607 Markt Holzkirchen

Phone:08024 2509

Hohenadel Bakery stands for traditional craftsmanship and high-quality baked goods. It offers freshly baked bread, rolls, cakes, and snacks every day. With a focus on regional ingredients and careful preparation, the bakery is a popular meeting place for connoisseurs in the region. Quality and customer proximity are top priorities here.

Bäckerei Kuhn ▼

Address: Am Lechlweg 1, 83607 Markt Holzkirchen

Phone:08024 6983

With over 100 years of history, this traditional bakery in Holzkirchen combines classic craftsmanship with the highest quality. The baked goods are mostly handmade using the finest ingredients, giving them an outstanding taste. The two-story building, with large windows offering a wide view, invites guests to relax in a cozy and stylish atmosphere.

Bäckerei Ratschiller ▼

Address: Marktplatz 15/ Heignkamerstraße 4 / Gewerbering 14b/ Bergfeldstraße 11, 83607 Markt Holzkirchen

Phone:08024 47490

Ratschillers Bakery stands for freshness, artisanal perfection, and exquisite baked goods made from the finest regional ingredients. With extensive experience and a passion for craftsmanship, recipes are carefully prepared to ensure the highest level of taste. Strict quality control guarantees that both delivery customers and visitors to the bakery’s branches can enjoy the best baked goods every day.
