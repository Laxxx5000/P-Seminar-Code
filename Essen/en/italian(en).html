Italian and Mediterranean

Here are some Italian restaurants in Holzkirchen that are worth a visit.

Il Crostino ▼

Adress: Münchner Str. 30, 83607 Markt Holzkirchen

Phone: 08024 4662285

At Il Cristino, guests can enjoy Italian cuisine in a modern and cozy atmosphere. The homemade pasta from the in-house pasta workshop and pizzas made according to a traditional family recipe with the finest mozzarella are highlights of the menu. The selection is complemented by delicious appetizers, soups, salads, and desserts – a true delight for lovers of Italian cuisine.

Ristorante da Franco ▼

Adress: Marienstraße 3b, 83607 Markt Holzkirchen

Phone: 08024 92769

Since 1997, Ristorante da Franco has been delighting guests with freshly prepared, authentic Italian cuisine. Whether pasta, pizza, or other Italian specialties – visitors can enjoy delicious dishes in a cozy atmosphere. The wellstocked wine cellar perfectly complements the culinary experience. Many guests and regulars consider this restaurant the “best Italian place in the area.”

Ristorante da Tosto ▼

Adress: Münchner Straße 44, 83607 Markt Holzkirchen

Phone: 08024 91379

At Ristorante Da Tosto, guests can embark on a culinary journey through Mediterranean cuisine. With over 50 different wood-fired pizzas, baked traditionally and topped with fresh ingredients, there is plenty to choose from. In addition to pizzas, the restaurant also offers antipasti, delicious meat and fish dishes, and a variety of pasta options.

Zum Griechen ▼

Adress: Marktplatz 20, 83607 Markt Holzkirchen

Phone: 08024 49549

Since 1990, Zum Griechen has been serving fresh, traditional Greek dishes, prepared with highquality ingredients and family recipes. Whether meat, fish, or vegetarian specialties – there is something for everyone. Children are warmly welcomed with a special kids’ menu. Greek hospitality in a cozy atmosphere.

Terra ▼

Adress: Münchner Str. 22, 83607 Markt Holzkirchen

Phone: 08024 46338181

Terra specializes in high-quality beef from Argentina and the USA. The menu features fresh steaks, Mediterranean appetizers, and handmade pasta. A house specialty is the “Hot Rock,” where guests can finish cooking their steak on a hot granite stone. A place for top-tier cuisine in a relaxed atmosphere.
