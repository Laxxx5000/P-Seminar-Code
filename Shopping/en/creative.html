Hagebaumarkt ▼

Adress: Industriestraße 9, 83607 Holzkirchen

Phone: 08024 9090

The Hagebaumarkt store in Holzkirchen offers a wide range of Arts and Crafts materials as well as professional building materials. The focus is on building and renovation, garden, tools and machines, as well as bathroom and sanitary products. Seasonal offers, such as winter supplies, are also available. The store also offers practical services such as wood cutting and advice on DIY projects. It is a popular centre for hobby and professional craftsman in the region.

Roland Strohmeier ▼

Adress: Münchner Str. 45, 83607 Holzkirchen

Phone: 08024 91960

Schreib- und Spielwaren Karner in Holzkirchen offers a wide range of stationery writing materials and toys. The store is known for its individual range and customer-oriented service. In addition to the usual writing materials, there are also toys for children like board games and puzzles. This store is a central point of contact in the region for school and craft supplies as well as gift ideas for every age group.

Bücherecke ▼

Adress: Münchner Straße 40, 83607 Holzkirchen

Phone: 08024 5152

The Holzkirchner Bücherecke has been a permanent institution in the cultural life of the market town of Holzkirchen for over four decades. It was founded in 1983 by Edith Wüster and Cornelia Engl and has remained a place for literature, encounters and personal advice to this day. The bookshop is located in a historic building from the 19th century, which used to house a print shop - where, among other things, the first Holzkirchen newspaper was printed. One of the printing presses from that time is now on display at the Deutsches Museum in Munich.
The range of books in the Bücherecke is varied and carefully selected: In addition to current novels, non-fiction and how-to books, there are also numerous books for children and young people as well as new publications of all genres. The focus is on personal contact and individual book recommendations - so the Bücherecke is not just a store, but a meeting place for everyone who loves books.
