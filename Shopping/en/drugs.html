Lebensmittel & Gesundheit

Rewe or Edeka, which will you choose? Or do you have a broken leg?

Rewe ▼

Adress: Tegernseer Str. 14, 83607 Holzkirchen

Phone: 08024 473458

The Rewe grocery store in Holzkirchen on Tegernseer Straße offers a modern shopping with a wide range of products. In addition to fresh fruit, vegetables and meat products, there is a large selection of dairy products, baked goods and frozen foods. Customers can also find organic products, vegan alternatives and specialties for a gluten- or lactose-free diet. REWE's private brands complement the range at a reasonable price. The friendly atmosphere, clean store are particularly appreciated.

Edeka ▼

Adress: Münchner Str. 62, 83607 Holzkirchen

Phone: 08024 3030093

The EDEKA grocery store in Holzkirchen offers a wide selection of fresh food, including meat, sausage and baked goods. They also offer gluten- and lactose-free products, as well as vegan and vegetarian products and emphasizes high quality and freshness.

Seemüller Apotheke ▼

Adress: Münchner Str. 56A, 83607 Holzkirchen

Phone: 08024 6165

The Seemüller Apotheke (pharmacy) in the HEP offers comprehensive pharmaceutical services. The great customer-oriented services is shown by rapid availability of medicines, the option of ordering by phone, digitally or on site and free delivery service. The pharmacy also offers support wit specific treatment. The team strives to ensure optimal care and support

Marien Apotheke ▼

Adress: Tegernseer Str. 1, 83607 Holzkirchen

Phone: 08024 92727

The Marien Apotheke in Holzkirchen offers a wide range of prescription and over-the-counter medicines as well as numerous health services. These include the reantal of breast pumps, inhalation devices and baby scales, the fitting of compression socks, as well as travel medicine.

Engel Apotheke ▼

Adress: Tölzer Str. 12, 83607 Holzkirchen

Phone: 08024 6850

The Engle Apotheke (pharmacy) in Holzkirchen offers a comprehensive range of medicines, including a prescription service and advice on individual health issues. It places particular emphasis on personal care and also offers natural healing products and health products. Customers appreciate the advice on site.

Artrium Gesundheitszentrum ▼

Adress: Münchner Str. 56, 83607 Holzkirchen

Phone: 08024 475420

The Atrium Gesundheitszentrum in Holzkirchen is the centre location for health, wellness and medical services. It houses a group practice with a focus on oncology as well as other specialities that provide comprehensive medical care.
In addition to medical services, the Medifit Centre, which specializes in physiotherapy treatments, massages and sports services. The Atrium also houses a pharmacy offering advice and medication.
