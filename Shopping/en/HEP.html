Holzkirchner Einkaufsparadies (HEP)

You'll find everything here, maybe even the love of your life!

HEP Einkaufszentrum ▼

Adress: Rosenheimer Str. 21, 83607 Holzkirchen

The HEP, also known as "Holzkirchner Einkaufsparadies" or "Shoppingparadise of Holzkirchen" is a modern Shopping center in Holzkirchen, that offers a great variety of stores and a variety of restaurants.
The grocery store EDEKA is the key component and provides with its extensive product range for the daily needs.
The DM Drug store complements the offer with cosmetic and care products.
Fashion interested people can find a variety of clothing at Takko Fashion, Ersting's Family and Jeans Fritz, while Deichmann offers shoes for every matter.
For sports enthusiasts Intersport Berauer offers high quality sports gear.
The store Depot offers decorations and small furniture and the store Expert offers a wide range of electronics.
The offer is complemented by the Seemüller pharmacy, which sells health products. There is also a store, which offers lottery and tobacco products.
Culinary delights are provided by the Canton Chinese restaurant with Asian specialities and the In Centro café for snacks and coffee specialties.
The Wünsche bakery in the EDEKA area offers freshly baked goods.
The HEP is a family-friendly central meeting point in Holzkirchen with free parking and a variety of stores.
