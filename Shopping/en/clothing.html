Modehaus Jennerwein ▼

Adress: Marktpl. 3, 83607 Holzkirchen

Phone: 08024 99980

The Modehaus Jennerwein or "Jennerwein fashion house" in Holzkirchen offers a big variety of Ladie's and men's fashion as well as underwear and hosiery. With brans such as Tommy Hilfiger amd Street One, it offers trendy clothing for every occasion.

Hut und Tracht ▼

Adress: Marktpl. 9, 83607 Holzkirchen

Phone: 08024 7886

"Hut und Tracht in Holzkirchen is a specialist store for traditional fashion near the market square. It offers high-quality traditional clothing for men and women as well as accessories from renowned brands such as Meindl and Lodenfrey, complemented by personal advice.
