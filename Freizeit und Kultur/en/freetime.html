Leisure facilities

Here you can work out whether on the ball, the machine, the board or in the water!

BaTuSa ▼

Adress: Baumgartenstraße 12, Holzkirchen

Phone: 08024 904440

This swimming pool offers something for everyone: an exciting slide for action and fun, a warm water pool for relaxation, a 25-meter main pool for athletes, and a lovingly designed children’s pool. The diving board is a special highlight for the brave! A great place for families and friends to have fun and unwind.

Skaterpark ▼

Adress: Am Ladehof, Holzkirchen

This skatepark is a paradise for skaters of all experience levels. It offers a variety of obstacles, including ramps, rails, and halfpipes, creating the perfect conditions for practicing tricks and having fun. A greplace for those who love adrenaline and want to improve their skills!

Jugendfreizeitanlage ▼

Adress: Am Ladehof, Holzkirchen

Located next to the skatepark, this modern recreational area offers a multifunctional sports field for street soccer, basketball, and volleyball. A grass area with slacklineposts and a small fitness course provide additional fun. The partly covered seating area is perfect for relaxing. A versatile spot for active leisure time!

Eisstadion ▼

Adress: Thanner Straße 6, Holzkirchen

Phone: 08024 9044-38

Located next to the skatepark, this modern recreational area offers a multifunctional sports field for street soccer, basketball, and volleyball. A grass area with slacklineposts and a small fitness course provide additional fun. The partly covered seating area is perfect for relaxing. A versatile spot for active leisure time!

MAX Gym ▼

Adress: Raiffeisenstraße 2-4, Holzkirchen

Phone: 08024 608371

This fitness studio offers a great selection of modern equipment and diverse training options. Whether strength training, endurance, or functional workouts, you’ll find everything you need for an effective workout. Suitable for both beginners and experienced athletes!

Sportplatz ▼

Adress: Baumgartenstraße, Holzkirchen

This sports field offers perfect conditions for various activities. It features a modern artificial turf field, a hard court, a beach volleyball court, and a running track. A practical water dispenser ensures athletes stay refreshed during training or games. A great place for teams and individual athletes!
