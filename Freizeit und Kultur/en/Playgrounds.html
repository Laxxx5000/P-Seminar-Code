Playgorunds

Fun for young and old!

Flinspachspielplatz ▼

Adress: Flinspacher Straße, Holzkirchen

Phone: 08024 904440

This versatile playground allows children to learn traffic rules while riding their bikes on specially designed streets. A small soccer field invites exciting matches, while the zip line provides thrilling experiences. A perfect place for movement, learning, and lots of fun!

Abenteuerspielplatz ▼

Adress: Otto-Mair-Ring, Holzkirchen

This playground excites with a long slide, an impressive climbing tree for little adventurers, a soccer field for sports activities, and a sandbox for digging and creativity. There’s something for everyone!

Spielplatz am Herdergarten ▼

Adress: Herdergarten, Holzkirchen

This idyllic playground features a small fountain, a spacious meadow, and large trees, creating a perfect spot for playing, relaxing, and enjoying nature. Ideal for little explorers and peaceful outdoor moment

Spielplatz an der Erlkamer Straße ▼

Adress: Erlkamer Straße, Holzkirchen

This playground offers thrilling experiences with a long zip line, a challenging climbing frame, and an impressive chestnut tree that provides shade in the summer and invites children to marvel at nature. A perfect spot for little adventurers!

Spielplatz am Lechelweg ▼

Adress: Lechlweg, Holzkirchen

This playground features a varied climbing frame, a fun slide, and a cozy meadow for playing and relaxing. A special bonus: an ice cream shop nearby provides the perfect refreshment on warm days!
