Churches

Wanna rock baroque, "Atomkraftwerk" or Dodekacon?

Segenskirche (ev.) ▼

Adress: Marktpl. 3, 83607 Holzkirchen

Phone: 08024 99980

The Segenskirche in Holzkirchen is an important place of worship that blends harmoniously into the townscape. As part of the Evangelical Lutheran parish of Holzkirchen, it serves as a central location for church services, devotions and a variety of community events. Its architectural style, which is often characterized by clear lines and an inviting atmosphere, invites visitors and worshippers alike to linger and reflect. The church is not only a place of faith, but also a cultural meeting place that plays an active role in shaping community life in Holzkirchen.

St.Laurentius (cath.) ▼

Adress: Marktplatz 8C, Holzkirchen

This Catholic church is centrally located on the market square in the heart of Holzkirchen. As a defining landmark of the town center, it attracts both worshippers and visitors. Its central location makes it a lively hub of community life, surrounded by stores, cafes and the hustle and bustle of the market. The church combines spirituality and everyday life in an impressive way and is a symbol of Holzkirchen's cultural and religious identity.

St. Joseph (cath.) ▼

Adress: St.-Josef-Straße 14, 83607 Holzkirchen

The Catholic church is located in the middle of a quiet residential area and is right next to a kindergarten. This harmonious neighborhood underlines its role as a place of encounter and community. Surrounded by green spaces and away from the hustle and bustle of the city, the church offers a peaceful retreat for prayer and reflection.
